Step 3

Plant-Based Purification + Microbial Electricity

The heart of our dual-purpose system

Our system uses Canna indica plants in a specially designed setup that combines phytoremediation with a Plant Microbial Fuel Cell (PMFC). The plants' roots absorb contaminants while microbes in the soil break down organic matter, generating electricity in the process.

Phytoremediation

The Canna indica plant naturally filters pollutants from water through its extensive root system, absorbing nitrogen, phosphorus, and heavy metals.

Microbial Fuel Cell

Bacteria in the soil around the plant roots break down organic matter, releasing electrons that are captured by an anode buried in the rhizosphere.

Electron Transfer

Electrons flow from the anode to the cathode through an external circuit, generating usable electricity while completing the water purification process.
